Privacy Policy for PassCheck

Last updated: June 2026

PassCheck is a password strength analyzer and password generator designed to operate entirely on your device.

Information Collection and Use

PassCheck does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information.

Passwords entered into the application are processed locally on your device and are not sent to any server.

Data Storage

PassCheck does not store passwords, personal information, or generated passwords on external servers.

Any information entered into the application remains on your device.

Third-Party Services

PassCheck does not use third-party analytics, advertising services, or tracking technologies.

Children's Privacy

PassCheck does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

Security

Because PassCheck operates locally on your device and does not transmit password data, your information remains under your control.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer through the Google Play Store listing.
